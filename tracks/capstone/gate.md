# Capstone - Your Project, Your Terms Gate

## Purpose

Verify ownership: the learner can choose, shape, finish, and explain a project on their own terms.

## Required artifact

Submit the track project plus supporting evidence: tests or checks, written explanation, self-evaluation, and at least one debugging or revision note.

## Pass criteria

- The work behaves as claimed
- The learner can explain the major decisions
- The learner can identify edge cases or risks
- The learner can revise or extend the work in a small unfamiliar direction
- The learner can describe what evidence proves readiness for the next track

## Outcomes

**Pass:** The learner is ready to continue.

**Revise:** The learner is close, but must strengthen missing evidence, unclear explanation, or incomplete verification.

**Repeat:** The learner should revisit the track if the core skill is not yet usable without heavy support.
