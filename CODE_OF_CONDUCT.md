# Code of Conduct

This curriculum is designed to be a respectful learning space for people from many backgrounds and starting points.

## Our standards

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give constructive feedback focused on the work.
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully.
- Preserve learner agency instead of taking over the work.
- Focus on what is best for the learning community.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing another person's private information without permission
- Dismissive or shame-based comments toward beginners
- Disruptive conduct that makes the community less safe or useful
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional learning space

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, issues, or other participation that violates this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Report issues through the GitHub issue tracker or by contacting the project maintainer directly.
